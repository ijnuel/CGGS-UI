Our Mission

At Chosen Generation Schools, our mission is to raise a generation of critical thinkers, compassionate leaders, and responsible global citizens. We believe every child carries extraordinary potential — our role is to ignite it.

Founded with a vision to bridge academic excellence with strong moral values, CGGS has grown into one of the most trusted educational institutions in the region, nurturing students from nursery through secondary school.

To be the leading center of learning excellence — where students don't just pass exams but discover their purpose, develop their character, and are empowered to positively impact their communities and the world beyond.
