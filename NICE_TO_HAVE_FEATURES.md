# Nice-to-Have Features for School Management System

This document contains additional feature suggestions specifically tailored for a school management system like CGGS-UI.

## Student Management Features

### 1. Student Attendance Tracking
- **Daily attendance marking** with visual indicators (Present/Absent/Late)
- **Attendance reports** by student, class, or date range
- **Attendance statistics** and trends
- **Parent notifications** for absences
- **Bulk attendance marking** for entire classes
- **Attendance calendar view** with color coding

### 2. Student Health Records
- **Medical information** storage (allergies, medications, conditions)
- **Emergency contact** management
- **Health incident reports** and tracking
- **Vaccination records** and reminders
- **Medical clearance** for activities

### 3. Student Behavior & Discipline
- **Incident reporting** system
- **Disciplinary actions** tracking
- **Merit/demerit points** system
- **Behavior reports** and analytics
- **Parent communication** for behavior issues

### 4. Student Portfolios
- **Digital portfolios** for student work
- **Achievement badges** and certificates
- **Photo galleries** of student activities
- **Progress tracking** over time
- **Student self-reflection** entries
